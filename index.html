About Me

Hey World, I am Devansh Bordia, a Security Engineer with a passion for finding and fixing security vulnerabilities.

With over 4 years of professional experience, I specialize in penetration testing, vulnerability research, and helping development teams build secure software. I've conducted 100+ security assessments across web applications, mobile platforms, cloud infrastructure, and DevSecOps pipelines.

My work has resulted in discovering 500+ vulnerabilities including critical issues like Full Account Takeovers, SSRF, Privilege Escalation, and SQL Injection across numerous enterprise applications. I've been recognized with 4 published CVEs and was awarded Bugcrowd MVP Q2 2020.
